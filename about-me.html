Why web-development?

My interest in software began last year, when I wanted improve efficieny in the department I worked in. I wrote a couple of simple scripts in Python, automated a couple of processes, and the further I dived into the world of software, the more the passion grew. I had been feeling like I was ready for a challange, so made the decision to actively pursue a software career. After much reasearch, I decided that web development would suit me the most. The front end development is fun because you get to see your work come to life on the web, whilst back end development resonates with me as a mechanical engineer who likes working on engine under the bonnet so to speak.

Why an apprenticeship?

Having completed an apprenticeship already, I know first-hand that apprenticeships are a great way to learn and a great way to tranisition into a new career. I learnt the most in my apprenticeship by working on the job whilst supplementing that with attending college.
